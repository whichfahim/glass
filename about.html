Get to know us

Behold the brains behind Glass-Bushra!

Like every other girl, Bushra's skin was breaking out and no products were working for her. While studying at UCL, she decided to look into skincare formulation and came up with a formula that helped her improve her skin.

While the ingredients were all natural, she realized they weren't available in her home country, Bangladesh. She wanted to help others achieve their ideal skin and she worked to make it happen.

And so in December 2018, Glass Cosmetics was born!
